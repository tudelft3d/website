Point Cloud Processing 2018

March 13th, 2018 · Delft, the Netherlands

Point Cloud Processing 2018 is a 1-day seminar on the latest developments in point cloud processing. It was held on 13 March 2018 at the Delft University of Technology organised by the NWO-funded project 3D for environmental modelling (3D4EM).

PCP2018 was a joint event with the NWO funded project 3DSM, ERC, the VOLTA project, EuroSDR and Geonovum and is organised by Ravi Peters, Jantien Stoter and Hugo Ledoux of the 3D geoinformation group at TU Delft.

The objective of this seminar is share knowledge about exciting developments in point cloud processing. Topics of interest are:

  • acquisition & visualisation
  • data structures & management,
  • segmentation & classification,
  • object detection & reconstruction,
  • applications & use-cases from practice.
