About the event

This 2-day event brings together different stakeholders — cities, industry, researchers, and developers — to share experiences and discuss the challenges and potential of semantic 3D city models as hubs of harmonised information, with a specific focus on urban energy modelling and energy-related applications in the built environment.

The ultimate goal is to better understand user needs and to strengthen multidisciplinary collaboration between urban energy modelling and 3D city modelling experts, while also touching on the emerging role of Artificial Intelligence in this domain.

This event is part of the International Workshop on the CityGML Energy ADE, hosted by the 3D Geoinformation group at TU Delft. The CityGML Energy ADE is an internationally recognised extension of CityGML, providing standardised features and attributes to support energy-related applications such as building energy simulation at city scale.
