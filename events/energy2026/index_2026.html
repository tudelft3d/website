We are organising a 2-day event on June 22–23, 2026. Monday 22 June (afternoon) will be a public dissemination event open to a broad audience. Tuesday 23 June (morning) will be a more technical developer meeting. Together, this constitutes an open  ”Knowledge exchange event about energy and the digital built environment”. Venues for both days will be announced soon.

The purpose of this event is to bring together different stakeholders (cities, users, scientists, etc.) to share experiences and discuss about challenges and potentials of semantic 3D city models as hubs of harmonised information. The specific focus will on urban energy modelling, and other energy-related applications dealing with the built environment.

The ultimate goal is to better understand user needs and to strengthen multidisciplinary collaboration between urban energy modelling and 3D city modelling experts, while also touching on the emerging role of Artificial Intelligence in this domain.
 
This event is part of the International Workshop on the CityGML Energy ADE, hosted by the 3D Geoinformation group at TU Delft on June 22–23, 2026. The CityGML Energy ADE is an internationally recognised extension of CityGML, providing standardised features and attributes to support energy-related applications such as building energy simulation at city scale.

The workshop, in general, and the knowledge exchange event, in particular, represent therefore a great opportunity to get in touch with experts from different domains. Don't miss it!
